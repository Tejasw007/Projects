Welcome to Attendance Manager

Choose your role to access the attendance management system. Teachers can generate QR codes for attendance sessions, while students can mark their presence using attendance codes.

Teacher Access

Generate secure QR codes for attendance sessions, manage subject-specific attendance records, and download Excel reports for your classes.

  • Generate QR codes for attendance
  • Download Excel attendance reports
  • Manage multiple subjects

Student Access

Mark your attendance quickly using the attendance code provided by your teacher. Simple, fast, and secure attendance marking process.

  • Quick attendance marking
  • Real-time validation
  • No account required

How It Works

Simple 3-step process for efficient attendance management

1

Teacher Generates QR

Teacher selects subject, authenticates, and generates a unique QR code with attendance code

2

Students Scan/Enter Code

Students scan QR code or manually enter the attendance code along with their details

3

Automatic Recording

Attendance is automatically recorded with timestamp and saved to Excel file
